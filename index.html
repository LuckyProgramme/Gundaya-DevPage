Biography

Hello, I'm Mervin Howel Gundaya, a passionate individual with a flair for technology and a love for exploration. Originally from the beautiful region of Bicol, I have found my current home in the vibrant city of Paranaque.


Hobbies/Interest

When I'm not immersed in the world of coding, you can find me engaging in a variety of activities that bring joy to my life. One of my favorite pastimes is playing games, where I can unleash my competitive spirit and challenge myself in virtual worlds. Additionally, music has a special place in my heart, and I find solace in its melodies. Whether it's jamming to my favorite tunes or exploring new genres, music has a way of soothing my soul and inspiring my creativity.


Education

Driven by my passion for technology, I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the University of the East, Manila Campus. This educational journey has been an exciting one, allowing me to delve deeper into the intricate workings of the digital world. With each passing day, I am gaining valuable knowledge and skills that will help shape my future career in the field of computer science.


Skills

With a strong foundation in programming, I have honed my skills in various languages that form the backbone of modern technology. Proficient in Python, SQL, Java, and C++, I have the ability to bring ideas to life through code. However, I never settle for complacency and constantly seek to expand my skill set. Currently, I am delving into the world of web development and familiarizing myself with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. By pushing the boundaries of my knowledge, I aim to stay at the forefront of technological advancements and contribute to innovative solutions in the digital landscape.
